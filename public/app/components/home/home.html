Why Choose Us ?

Most of the modern-day learning techniques are focused on visual learning -- reading, black boards, white boards. Yet many people, such as auditory learners, students with reading or sight challenges, learners with dyslexia are left behind by this approach. elev8 is made to address this issue: an interactive learning tool that delivers content aurally.

elev8 Flashcards takes the traditional flashcard learning experience and makes it interactive and voice based. Content can be created from scratch, shared with other users. This provides a much better experience for users, specifically those that are studying alone or have difficulty reading. Additionally, the interactive style of elev8 allows learners to request hints or delve deeper into topics to learn more.
